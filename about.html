ABOUT

Modern computers run code in parallel. Real-life tasks benefit from parallelism. But... so far, oding games had only sequential instructions! Blockly Par is a game inspired in the Blockly-Games platform. It introduces parallel thinking through a game involving day-to-day tasks and parallel coding instructions. Blockly Par is specially targeted to freshman students in Computer Science majors. We expect that, at the end of the game, the students feel challenged to explore parallelism in their programs.

This project was developed during a Bachelor thesis in Computer Science at the Federal University of Santa Maria, in Brazil.
