ABOUT PARALLEL PROGRAMMING CODE

ABOUT

Blockly Par is a game inspired in Blockly-Games, that uses Blockly programming language to teach parallel thinking through a game. It has educational purposes, and was designed specially to freshman students in Computer Science majors. Is expected that at the end of the games the students feel challenged to explore parallelism in their programs

This project was developed during as a Bachelor thesis in Computer Science at the Federal University of Santa Maria, in Brazil.
